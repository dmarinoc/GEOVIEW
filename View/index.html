Socio-economic Indicators


What is "Sistema Integrado de Indicadores Sociales del Ecuador â€“ SIISE"?
It is a system that provides and publish statistical information available of Ecuador, that represents a integral group of social indicators.
The information about each indicator is shown with chronological series of the evolution over life conditions; besides this information is presented as tables with the respective conceptual definition.
It is available around 1500 indicatores, with the most relevant information about social sectors. For more information you can visit: www.siise.gob.ec
